Traveller

No defects logged, and no defect process flow identified. No defect strategy identified, including the defect life cycle.

No use of Exploratory Testing.

Don't know what Exploratory Testing is or have investigated but not applied.

Artisan

Defects are logged as and when team sees it necessary. Defects are logged but not properly communicated to stakeholders.

Some basic adhoc use of Exploratory Testing.

No list of issues, bugs, list of features and/or notes on the testing conducted.

Expert

Defect strategy and process flow is identified and documented, and communicated to all stakeholders.

Exploratory Testing is considered as part of the testing effort and included as part of the estimate of a user story.

All Exploratory Test sessions are recorded and documented on a spreadsheet or some other tool.

Professional

A tool is used to record issues/incidents/defects.

High priority defects that are carried forward are reported, tracked and tested whenever fixed.

Defects are prioritised and re-tested according to their priority.

Use of a Test Charter for exploratory session to record and document the testing.

Exploratory techniques are recommended and used, but there is still room for improvement.

Exploratory testing does not overlap basic Functional tests already covered and are reported in terms of Test Coverage metrics.

Master

All defects are properly logged, and all stakeholders are aware of the defect process and it is being followed accordingly.

All defects are properly fixed, and re-tested.

Use of a Standardised Test Charter or Tool to record a proper Session Based Exploratory Test.

Exploratory techniques are fully used, strategies are fully documented and used by all stakeholders.

Estimation of work includes effort for automating tests that find bugs using Exploratory Testing techniques.
